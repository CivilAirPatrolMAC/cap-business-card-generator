Business Card Guidance

This tool allows Civil Air Patrol members and NHQ staff to generate compliant business cards using approved branding elements, grade conventions, and contact information.

Before you generate

  • Use approved CAP grade abbreviations, courtesy titles, and official naming conventions.
  • Include only duty positions, unit names, and contact information appropriate to your role.
  • Keep titles, unit names, and address lines concise so the layout remains readable.
  • Review spacing, alignment, and legibility before printing or distributing.

Supported Avery Templates

This layout is compatible with the following Avery® business card templates:

18871, 27871, 27881, 27883, 28371, 28877, 28878, 38871, 38873, 38876, 5371, 5376, 5377, 55871, 55876, 5870, 5871, 5874, 5876, 5877, 5911, 8371, 8376, 8377, 8471, 8870, 8871, 8873, 8875, 8876, 8877.

Grade & Name

Use approved CAP grade abbreviations. Do not use "SM" or add professional post-nominals.

Duty Position(s)

List duty positions and unit names exactly as you want them to appear on the card.

Address

Use line breaks only where needed to maintain readability.

Phone Number(s)

Phone numbers are automatically formatted with periods between number groups.

Email Address

Only official Civil Air Patrol email domains are accepted.

Only @xxwg.cap.gov, @xxwg.cap.us, @cap.gov, and @cap.us addresses are allowed.
