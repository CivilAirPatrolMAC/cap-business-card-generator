Business Card Guidance

This tool allows Civil Air Patrol members to generate compliant business cards using approved branding elements, grade conventions, and contact information.

Before you generate

  • Use approved CAP grade abbreviations and official naming conventions.
  • Include only duty positions, unit names, and contact information appropriate to your role.
  • Keep titles, unit names, and address lines concise so the layout remains readable.
  • Review spacing, alignment, and legibility before printing or distributing.
